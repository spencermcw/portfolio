About Us

Jersey's was established in November of 2007 with a specialization in hair care for men who seek a new experience.
We value relaxation and draft beer; our bar area is the perfect place to prepare for one of our expert stylists to give you the trim you deserve.
Enjoy watching your favorite team on one of our 8 high-definition TV's as you're receiving a first class cut from our highly trained staff.
Women are certainly welcome! Unfortunately we do not offer services such as perms and manicures/pedicures.
Jersey's is located on the corner of Mangrove Avenue and Valambrosa near the heart of Chico.
